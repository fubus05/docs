This guide walks you through adding a partner that uses a simple API key — the most common setup. For OAuth2, CSV, or custom authentication, see [Authentication methods](/partner-integration/auth-methods).

## Before you start

You need:
- An active AdsVector account
- Your affiliate program's **API endpoint URL**
- Your affiliate program's **API key** or access token

## Step 1: Open the Partners page

In the AdsVector dashboard, navigate to **Partners** and click **Add partner**.

## Step 2: Fill in the basic details

| Field | What to enter |
|-------|---------------|
| **Name** | A display name, e.g. `Fraga Affiliates` |
| **Slug** | A short unique ID, e.g. `fraga` — used in URLs and logs |
| **API URL** | The stats endpoint from your affiliate panel |
| **Auth method** | Select `API Key` |
| **API key** | Paste your key or token from the affiliate panel |

## Step 3: Save and run the first sync

Click **Save**. Then open the partner detail page and click **Sync now**.

AdsVector fetches data from the API, creates campaigns, and imports the last 7 days of stats. The sync usually takes a few seconds. Check the **Sync logs** tab for the result.

If you see `SUCCESS`, you're done. If you see `FAILED`, check the error message in the sync log — it usually points to an authentication issue or a wrong URL.

## Step 4: Enable auto-sync (optional)

On the partner detail page, set **Auto-sync interval**. The minimum is 1 minute; a value of 60–1440 minutes is typical for affiliate stats.

AdsVector will queue a sync job on that interval and backfill any data since the last successful sync (up to 30 days).
